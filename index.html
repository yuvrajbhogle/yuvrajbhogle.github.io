About Us

Medmarket is a free, Medicine shopping store website created as a last year project. This Website is Created for the project purpose under the guidence and allowance of the faculties of Parul Polytechnic Institute Information Technology Department.

This project is used mainly for medical store to maintain the detail of medical stores such as stock and accounting. This Medmarket website is design as to ease the work load of medical shop professionals. The main feature includes inventory and stock control, accounting, client management . This website helps you to track all the products of medical shop .its a medical shop accounting flexible and adaptive website suited for medical shops or store or pharmacies for analysis.

Medmarket is a store website for medical distribution and purchase By using this website the users can place order for the medicines they need and can have consultancy related to any medicines. They can also upload prescriptions for placing order.
